About Me!

How did I get here?

As you can read from the home page and the name of the website, my name is Mikolaj Rutyna. I was born in Poland and raised in the UK for most of my life. Over the years, I have developed an interest in the technical and the creative world. I have a deep passion for video games since childhood, which still resides within me to this day. Not only it fuels my dopamine, but I also gain new ideas and inspiration which is how I mainly ended up here.


So what kind of interests and skills have I gained?

• Game development in Roblox (formerly) and Unity.
• Front-end web development.
• Traditional Art:
▸ Landscapes and portraits involving media such as pencils, soft pastels and watercolouring.
• Digital Art:
▸ UI design - for websites and games.
▸ Pixel art - for games.
▸ GFX (Renders in Blender + Post-editing in paint.net)
▸ Graphics Design - mainly for thumbnails.
• Video editing:
▸ Stylised editing in a music video fashion.
▸ Trailers


Roblox? Really?

Yup. When I learned to code for the first time with Python at school, I used my knowledge on Roblox which has a built in engine using Lua. Because of Roblox was how I was easily able to transition to Unity due to the same principles in both engines.


What am I doing now?

Currently studying in Goldsmiths University doing Creative Computing. In the second year now where I have to start thinking about what kind of projects I will do this and next year. I am looking to find an internship to gain a beneficial experience for my future and my projects.
